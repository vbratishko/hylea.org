About HYLEA

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To facilitate the sustainable development of society, communities, and economy through multi-faceted scientific, advisory, project, and analytical activities in ecology, energy efficiency, and local governance.

Vision: To be a leading global think tank, transforming regional potential through scientific excellence, environmental stewardship, and cutting-edge innovations.

Core Expertise

  • Project & Grant Management (full cycle support)
  • Scientific & Advisory Services (Ecology, Bioenergy, Agrotechnologies)
  • Development of Communities and LGS (capacity building, digitalisation)
  • Policy Development and Legal Support
  • Integration of European Practices & EU Standards

Partnerships

European Integration & Best Practices

We actively facilitate Ukraine's European integration by adapting and implementing EU standards in policy, ecology, and municipal management, fostering strong partnerships between civil society and government bodies.

Strategic Partnership for Grant Projects

HYLEA actively seeks and contributes to international consortia focused on impactful research and innovation. We are ready to engage in full-scale proposal development for major EU programs, including Horizon Europe, LIFE, and Interreg.

Grant Focus Areas

Our scientific capacity aligns perfectly with the following key clusters:

  • Cluster 5: Climate, Energy, Mobility (especially renewables, bioenergy, and smart mobility).
  • Cluster 6: Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources (sustainable agriculture, circular economy, ecological restoration).
  • Digitalisation & Governance (e-governance, smart city solutions, data validation).

Scientific Contribution

We offer specialized scientific services critical for project success:

  • High-quality scientific and technical writing for proposals (WP Leaders/Task Leaders).
  • Regional data collection, validation, and socio-economic analysis (Ukraine context).
  • Pilot project implementation and technology demonstration in real-world settings.

Dissemination & Outreach

We ensure maximum project impact and reach across Ukraine and Eastern Europe:

  • Strong network with local authorities, universities, and professional associations.
  • Expertise in policy recommendation development and legislative analysis.
  • Organisation of large-scale international dissemination conferences and events.
